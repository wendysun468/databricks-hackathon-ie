# App Flow Scaffold

## Screen 1: Entry

- choose specialty or care type
- choose geography
- choose affordability or friction view

## Screen 2: Ranked Results

- show districts or clusters ranked by care friction
- show travel time to nearest trusted care
- show affordability label

## Screen 3: Detail View

- show nearby facilities
- show evidence snippets
- show trust labels
- show notes and shortlist actions

## Screen 4: Compare

- compare 2 or 3 candidate districts
- show tradeoffs between travel time, trust, and affordability
